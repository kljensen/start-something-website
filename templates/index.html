Start Something at Yale
with the Yale Entrepreneurial Institute

“Start Something” is a workshop where Yale students and faculty can learn the basic tools that entreprenuers use to launch new companies including:

  • the Lean Start-up method;
  • customer development; and,
  • finding product–market fit.
